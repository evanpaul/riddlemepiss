Q&A

Who wrote these terrible, nonsensical riddles?

A computer program. I scraped all of the riddles from riddles.com and used them to build a Markov chain. I asked it to generate me a bunch of riddles and selected the ones I found funny. I edited the punctuation (because it generates weirdly), but I left everything else unmodified.

Riddle me what?

This website is inspired by the segment "Riddle Me Piss" introduced in episode 418 of the hit podcast, My Brother, My Brother, and Me (an advice show for the modern era).

Are these dynamically generated?

Not exactly. My script generated all the riddles but what you see is manually selected. I didn't "host the model live" because it generates some unfunny, garbage riddles. Also because it takes more work and I'm lazy.
